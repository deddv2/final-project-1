            <h2>Key Facts About Michigan</h2>
            <p>Michigan is a unique state known for its rich history, automotive industry, and stunning natural
                landscapes.</p>

            <h3>State Highlights</h3>
            <ul>
                <li>Surrounded by four of the Great Lakes: Superior, Michigan, Huron, and Erie.</li>
                <li>The only state in the US split into two distinct peninsulas: the Upper and Lower Peninsulas.</li>
                <li>Known as the birthplace of the modern American automobile industry, centered in Detroit.</li>
            </ul>
